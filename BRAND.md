# MyOtherPair — Brand Guidelines

## Identity

**Name:** MyOtherPair

**Tagline:** *Every shoe deserves a match.*

**One-liner:** The peer-to-peer marketplace where people with one foot, mismatched sizes, or a single spare can buy and sell individual shoes.

**Mission:** Remove the economic and social barriers that force people to buy whole pairs when they only need one.

---

## Brand Personality

| Trait | Expression |
|-------|-----------|
| **Inclusive** | We never make disability or difference the punchline. We celebrate it. |
| **Practical** | Clear, direct language. No fluff. People here are solving a real problem. |
| **Warm** | Community-first. Use "you" and "your". Write like a knowledgeable friend. |
| **Quietly bold** | Confident without being loud. Let the concept do the talking. |

---

## Voice & Tone

**Do:**
- Write short, punchy sentences in headlines
- Use active voice
- Acknowledge the user's situation without infantilizing it
- Keep CTAs action-verbs: "Start selling", "Find your match", "List a shoe"

**Don't:**
- Use euphemisms or clinical language around disability
- Overpromise (no "game-changing" / "revolutionary")
- Use ableist idioms ("turn a blind eye", "fell on deaf ears")
- Use complex jargon

**Example copy — good:**
> "List the shoe that's been sitting in your closet. Someone out there needs exactly it."

**Example copy — avoid:**
> "Leverage our proprietary matching algorithm to monetize your pre-owned footwear assets."

## Typography

**Primary font:** System font stack (`-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif`)

When a custom font is added: use **Inter** (headings) + **Inter** (body) — clean, highly legible, open source.

| Role | Size | Weight | Letter-spacing |
|------|------|--------|----------------|
| Display / Hero | 4–5rem | 900 | −0.04em |
| Section heading | 2.25rem | 900 | −0.04em |
| Card heading | 1.125rem | 700 | 0 |
| Body | 1rem | 400 | 0 |
| Caption / Meta | 0.875rem | 400–500 | 0 |
| Badge / Label | 0.75rem | 700 | +0.08em uppercase |

## Logo

The logo is a wordmark: **MyOther** (accent orange) + **Pair** (ink dark). No space between words, weight 800+.

```
MyOtherPair
^^^^^^^  ^^^
F04E23  0F172A
```

**Usage:**
- Minimum size: 120px wide (digital), 30mm (print)
- Clear-space: equal to the height of the capital "O" on all sides
- Approved backgrounds: white, `#fafaf8`, `#1a1a2e` (invert to all-white on dark)
- Never stretch, rotate, recolor, or add effects to the wordmark

---

## Iconography & Imagery

**Icons:** Outline style, 1.5px stroke, rounded caps. Preferred set: Lucide Icons.

**Photography:**
- Real people, diverse bodies and abilities
- Natural light, unposed feel
- Avoid stock-photo aesthetics
- Show shoes in context (on feet, on floors, in hands) — not isolated product shots

**Illustrations:** Flat, geometric, two-to-three color max using brand palette. Playful but not childish.

## Naming Conventions (in-product)

| Concept | Term to use | Avoid |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| A shoe being sold | **listing** | product, item, SKU |
| User who lists | **seller** | vendor, merchant |
| User who buys | **buyer** | customer, consumer |
| Successful pairing | **match** | transaction (unless financial) |
| Someone with limb difference | **amputee** (if self-identified) or **person with a limb difference** | handicapped, disabled person |

---

## Social Media

- **Tone:** Warm, community-driven. Celebrate matches. Highlight real stories.
- **Hashtags:** `#MyOtherPair` `#EveryShoeDeservesAMatch` `#LimbDifference` `#AdaptiveFashion`
- **Do not:** Buy followers, use growth-hacking dark patterns, make disability the subject of humor.
